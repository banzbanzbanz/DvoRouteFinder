About the app



Route Finder Davao

  • Route Finder Davao help people who are new to jeepney routes and fare of Davao City.
  • The application features a list of jeepney routes that are easily understandable, it also has a origin and destination entry to calculate the user's location and give suggestions on what jeepney route to ride and its average amount of fare .
